Orryx Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-05-13

What we collect

  • Anonymous event data: event type, language, phase number, aggregated scores.
  • No names, emails, conversation text, or IP addresses.

Why we collect it

To understand how often Orryx is used and where users drop off, so we can improve the experience.

How we store it

Events are stored in encrypted Pipedream logs and a private database. Logs older than 12 months are purged automatically.
